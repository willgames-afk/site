How to play

Shoot the Enemy Spaceships while avoiding their fire to get POINTS!
Use W and S (Or arrows) to move and spam Space to shoot.

You can collect powerups, which refill one life. If you have all 3 lives, then it will give you a speed boost
There are also some caves up ahead, but I'm sure you'll be able to navigate them.
Finally, if you get going fast enough (from powerups or by entering a cave), you can ram through aliens spacecraft without taking any damage.
